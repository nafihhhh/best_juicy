Our Story

Founded in 2018, Fresh Squeeze began with a simple mission: to provide delicious, nutritious juices made from the freshest ingredients possible.

Our founder, Emily Chen, was frustrated by the lack of truly healthy juice options available. Most store-bought juices were filled with added sugars and preservatives. She decided to create a juice shop that would prioritize quality, nutrition, and taste above all else.

Today, we continue to handcraft each juice with care, using only organic fruits and vegetables sourced from local farms. We're proud to be a part of your health journey.
